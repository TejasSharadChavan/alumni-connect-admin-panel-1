## ğŸ“Š Features Overview

### 1. Influence Score (Top of Page)

**What You'll See**:

- Your total score out of 100
- Percentile ranking (e.g., "Top 10%")
- Score breakdown by category:
  - Mentorship (30 points max)
  - Jobs Posted (25 points max)
  - Referrals (20 points max)
  - Posts (15 points max)
  - Engagement (10 points max)
- Progress to next milestone

**What It Means**:

- Higher score = More impact on students
- Percentile shows your ranking among all alumni
- Each category shows your contribution level

---

### 2. Smart Recommendations Tab

**What You'll See**:

- **High Priority Matches** (70%+ match)
  - Students with best skill/branch alignment
  - Green badges showing match percentage
- **Good Matches** (50-70% match)
  - Students with solid alignment
  - Blue badges showing match percentage

**Actions You Can Take**:

- Click "Offer Mentorship" to connect with a student
- View student skills and branch
- See match percentage

**How Matching Works**:

- Compares your skills with student skills
- Considers branch alignment
- Factors in career interests
- Prefers recent graduates

---

### 3. Students Needing Help Tab

**What You'll See**:

- Students who sent mentorship requests
- Their topic of interest
- Branch and basic info

**Actions You Can Take**:

- Click "Reach Out" to initiate contact
- View student details
- See urgency (request date)

---

### 4. Referral Center Tab

**What You'll See**:

- **Highly Ready** (80%+ score)
  - Students with complete profiles
  - Multiple projects
  - Active job seekers
- **Ready** (65-80% score)
  - Strong candidates
  - Minor improvements needed

**Actions You Can Take**:

- Click "Resume" to view student's resume
- Click "Refer" to initiate referral
- See project count and application activity

**Readiness Score Based On**:

- Profile completeness (headline, bio, resume)
- Number of projects
- Job application activity
- Skills listed
- Academic year
